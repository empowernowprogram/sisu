Installation Instructions VR

  1. Click on the download button and save the apk file to your computer.
  2. Download Sidequest onto your desktop./
  3. Connect the Oculus to your PC using a USB cable.
  4. Open Sidequest.
  5. Download apk onto headset (Please follow instructions here)
  6. Select the downloaded apk. Sidequest message should say "Successfully uploaded"
  7. Start the application in your headset.
  8. Note: The application in the headset should be located the menu under "Unknown Sources" (under drop down in top right hand corner)
