A Closer Look at Our Trainings

HR Business Conduct Training

Help your employees learn how to respond to harassment, discrimination, bullying, and a hostile work environment

Why choose this training?

This training is designed to help employees learn to
handle a variety of negative situations with realistic scenarios.

This training focuses on common workplace issues such as
harassment, discrimination, and bullying.

Why does this matter? Studies show that 30% of employees do
not know how to handle such workplace issues and this type of
behavior can have profound psychological effects including
decreased productivity on the victim, increased turnover,
and even lawsuits.

Sisu VR helps companies empower their employees and be
more empathic.

Training Program Details

The program is divided into separate employee training modules*
Module 1 - Sexual Harrasment, Hostile Work Environment
Module 2 - Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, Quid Pro
Module 3 - Sexual Harrasment, Hostile Work Environment
Module 4 - Sexual Harrasment, Hostile Work Environment


*Admin, HR, and manager trainings are also available.

Active Shooter Training

Help your employees prepare and respond to an active shooter situation using the Run-Hide-Fight method from the Department of Homeland Security.

Why choose this training?

This training is designed to help civilians prepare for an active
shooter situation by providing them with an immersive training.

A virtual environment provides a controlled and secure setting for the
training to take place.

This training provides employees with a realistic scenario, allowing
them to practice and prepare for a real-life active shooter situation.

Let’s hope they never have to use it.

Training Program Details

What the Active Shooter Training covers*

  • • Firearm Sounds
  • • Listening Drill
  • • Run: Quick Escape 
  • • Hide: Cover vs conceal
  • • Fight: Improvised weapons
  • • Evacuation
