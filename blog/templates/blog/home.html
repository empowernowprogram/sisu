What is Sisu VR?


Sisu VR offers immersive and engaging virtual reality (VR) trainings to ensure your workplace
can be safe and harassment-free for your employees.

Our trainings focus on topics such as bullying, sexual harassment, Quid Pro Quo, and how to
respond if an active shooter enters the building.


Let’s work together to empower your workforce today.
