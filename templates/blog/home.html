The Problem

In the post-Harvey Weinstein world, responsible employers know sexual harassment has very severe consequence.

Starting from January 1, 2021, the state of California requires mandatory harassment training for employers with more than 5 employees.

Our Solution

“After experiencing the severe aftermath of workplace harassment, I founded Sisu VR to help professionals bring their best selves to work, everyday.

The Empower Now Program, delivers anti-harassment training through virtual reality. It will inspire empathy and empower workplaces, enabling all professionals to work fearlessly.”

- Jocelyn Tan, Sisu VR Founder & CEO

Empower Now Program (ENP)

Create an Empowered Workplace with:

Immersive Virtual Reality Experience

Engage in VR simulations based on real-life scenarios

Play Through Multiple Perspectives

Learn to handle high-stake situations as a complainant, offender, or observer.

Evidence-Based and Ethical Framework

Modules built on psychological reserach
and ethical frameworks
